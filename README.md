## Project Description
MindEase is a web application designed to help students monitor and improve their mental well-being. The platform provides a supportive and accessible environment where students can track their stress levels, access personalized resources, and seek professional guidance if needed.  

## Features
- **Emoji-based Stress Survey:** Students can complete a quick survey to assess their current anxiety and stress levels.  
- **Personalized Resources:** Based on the survey results, students receive tailored meditation videos, tips, and stress-relieving games.  
- **Professional Support:** Access emergency contact information and connect with available counsellors when needed.  

## Usage
Students can log in to MindEase and navigate through the following sections:  
1. **Survey** – Complete the stress survey to get personalized recommendations.  
2. **Resources** – Discover curated videos, games, and articles to manage stress.  
3. **Counsellors** – View available mental health professionals and book sessions.
