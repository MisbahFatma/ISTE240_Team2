About MindEase

MindEase is a web-based platform designed to help students manage stress and improve their mental well-being. Through this platform, students can complete quick stress surveys to track their current stress levels, discover personalized resources such as videos, tips, and games tailored to their needs, and access support from available counsellors. MindEase aims to provide a calm and supportive environment where students can take the first step toward better mental health.

How to Use

Users can navigate through MindEase easily: after logging in, students can access the survey section to input their stress levels and receive tailored recommendations. The resources page offers curated materials for managing stress, while the counsellor page allows students to view available professionals and book sessions directly. This structured flow ensures that students can monitor their mental health regularly, find helpful tools, and connect with support whenever needed.
